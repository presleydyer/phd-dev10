Restaurant Description
Established in 2019 in Wichita, Kansas, the home of its founder, Emporia Street Kitchen has strived to server the best quality American staple foods that satisfy your palette. We've done our research and have created a menu that will surely offer something for everyone. From our delicious burgers to our crisp and fresh salads, we here at Emporia Street Kitchen have something for whatever you're craving! Stop in when you're feeling hungry and we promise you will leave with a great experience and a full stomach!
